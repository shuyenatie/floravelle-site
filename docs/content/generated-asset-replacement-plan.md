# Generated Asset Replacement Plan

This preview round uses generated images as a visual bridge until real product and lifestyle photos are ready.

## Current policy

- Generated images are used for homepage atmosphere, collection thumbnails, product card visuals, and subpage hero banners.
- They are placeholders for presentation quality, not claims of exact current production output.
- Replace the homepage hero and primary product images first when real assets arrive.

## Priority order for replacement

1. Homepage hero and product detail hero
2. Core category icons
3. Best seller product cards
4. Story section lifestyle images
5. Subpage hero banner

## Recommended real-photo replacements

- Homepage hero:
  One strong interior scene with your best faux blossoms in a clean vase and premium home setting.
- Product cards:
  White or pale neutral background, consistent crop, full product visible, one SKU per frame.
- Product detail support:
  Add one close-up of petals or leaf texture and one styling shot in a vase.
- OEM / factory:
  Prefer clean sample room, packaging, QC, carton, or showroom images over workshop clutter.
